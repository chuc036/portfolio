Omniuser Div is a jewelry collection for the digital citizens of the world.
As a part of my accessories course in late 2019, we were asked to make a line luxury accessories. I wanted to create something that would bridge my interests in digital culture and visual presentation. With "Big Tech's" presence the united states, I felt like bringing the abstract concept of the web to a more physical form. We all engage with the basic design structures of the web, like divs, pixels, and various forms of multimedia. I was inspired by the iconic graphics for apple by Susan Kare. The pieces are hand-soldered Swarovski crystal cupchain.
