Commandment Generator Prints
Print posters for semester 1 of thesis (2020)
When corporate cable began to croak from the migration to streaming, it enabled more production of film and television content. Around a half decade later, the work being produced usually pushed genre conventions to avoid similarities. What happens when that repetitive process begins to collapse in on itself? What form does late-stage streaming production take- Does it forsake the moving image? Will it rely on text and graphic elements in an increased way in order to avoid convolution?
